Partially inspired by Aicosu's Modern Thedas AU fic Message Sent and by the webseries The Guild.

As always:
Elvhen names came from FenxShiral's fantastic Project Elvhen: Book of Names.
Other bits of Elvish from either FenxShiral's Project Elvhen: Expanding the Elvhen Language or Project Elvhen: An Elvhen Lexicon
Lesser known bits of elvish are translated in the end notes

This fic now has a companion Wiki/Reference work ( An Encyclopedia of Thedas ) that goes more in-depth with world building/lore than I could go in the fic, which receives semi-regular updates. Updates are mentioned at the beginning of chapters of this work as they are completed.
