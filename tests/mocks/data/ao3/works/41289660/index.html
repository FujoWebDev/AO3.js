Dear Makarov Dreyar,

As you know the government in our world stinks. The Ministry of magic refuses to believe Voldemort has returned from the dead. So now we don't have proper defenses for them. The school is still safe, but this is Voldemort we are talking about! I wish to ask for your help. Please come here to protect our school. It is extra dangerous for the students: Harry, Ron, and Hermione. Voldemort wants Harry dead. Ron is wanted by his followers for reasons unknown. And Hermione Granger is his child. For their protection I beg for your help! 

-Albus Dumbledore

Dear Albus Dumbledore,

My guild will be glad to help for a year at most. That's as much as I can do without having our own dark mage Zeref following us. To keep the government off our backs send a flyer for a request, old friend. I am having Lucy, Natsu, and Gray look after them for you if you wish. They are a group of trouble magnetics themselves. Lucy is wanted dead or to become the demon of Zeref. Gray is an undercover mage. And Natsu is Zeref's brother. We will arrive in 5 days. Here is very important information...

-Makarov Dreyar
